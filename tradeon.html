Welcome to TradeOn! We value your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the TradeOn application.

When you use the TradeOn app, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Username, email, phone number 📧📱
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, details of goods/services you wish to exchange 🛍️
  • Transaction Information: Records of exchanges you make 🔄

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide services and improve app functionality 📈
  • Connect users who wish to exchange goods and services 🔗
  • Customize your user experience based on your preferences 🎯
  • Indicate the location of goods for exchange decisions 📍
  • Display product details to aid in decision-making 📊
  • Show images of products to help with decision-making 🖼️

3. Sharing Information with Third Parties

TradeOn will not sell or share your personal information with third parties. However, we may share information in the following cases:

  • Compliance with legal requirements and government regulations ⚖️

4. Your Rights Regarding Personal Information

You can:

  • Access and edit your information in your profile page ✏️
  • Request deletion of your information from our system 🧹
  • Adjust privacy settings, such as turning off notifications or limiting data sharing ⚙️

5. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data and use the following protective measures:

  • Data encryption – Information transmitted and stored is encrypted to prevent unauthorized access 🔐
  • Access control – Only authorized personnel can access sensitive information 🚪
  • Abnormal behavior detection – Our systems monitor for potentially harmful activities and prevent unauthorized access ⚠️
  • Data backup – Regular backups to reduce the risk of data loss 💾
  • Adherence to security standards – We use industry standards like HTTPS and data encryption to protect your information 🔒

Although we take stringent security measures, no system is 100% secure. We recommend using a strong password, avoiding sharing personal information, and regularly updating the app 🔑
